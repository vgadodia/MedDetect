Med Detect is a machine learning automatic disease detection and user management system targetted towards hospitals and medical clinics, that enables them to instantly diagnose diseases such as malaria from blood samples, with a high accuracy. Our system collects basic information and an image, and then returns quick and accurate results. We use advanced machine learning algorithms to classify the user uploaded image into uninfected (normal) or parasitized (diagnosed with malaria), and then automatically generate a CSV medical report. We also have a patient data management system, as well as a data visualisation page.

Try it out!

Why Med Check?

Med Check significantly reduces the human resource and expertise required for diagnosing malaria and makes the entire system far more efficient and less prone to error. Med Check would have a huge impact in rural areas in nations like India, where there is acute lack of qualified doctors to treat such diseases.
